About Us

Reading Cafe is an emerging literary publisher in Sri Lanka committed to promote Sri Lankan Writings in to the world. As a country rich with a remarkable history, we put our full efforts to deliver great writings that present glimpses of Sri Lankan literature . Our main scope is to provide publications that impact Sri Lankan readers' literary skills, particularly books that can instil good values among readers. Our publishing portfolio contains books and products for readers of all ages at every stage of life. Our list of authors includes writers of all stages, from emerging writing talents to award-winning authors. Thus, we ensure that the audience abundantly hears their voices.

At Reading Cafe, we're passionate about connecting readers with their next great adventure. Explore our curated collection of books spanning genres, from timeless classics to contemporary bestsellers. Immerse yourself in the world of literature, sip on the rich blend of stories, and savor the joy of reading. Join us in this literary journey, where every page turns into a delightful experience. Cheers to a cup of coffee and a world of stories at Reading Cafe!"

Mission

"Empowering minds through the magic of literature, our mission at Reading Cafe is to create a vibrant online space where readers of all tastes and ages can discover, embrace, and share the transformative power of books. We strive to foster a community that celebrates diversity in storytelling, connecting readers with narratives that inspire, challenge, and entertain."

Vision

"As we look ahead, Reading Cafe envisions becoming a premier online destination, not just for buying books but for cultivating a global reading culture. We aspire to be a platform that transcends borders, bringing together book enthusiasts to exchange ideas, explore new perspectives, and collectively build a more enlightened and connected world through the love of reading."
